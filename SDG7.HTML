SDG - 7 & Indian Traditions


**Conserving Energy**

:

Practise energy conservation at home by turning off lights, appliances, and electronic devices when not in use. This helps reduce energy consumption.


**Use of Natural Light**:

Make use of natural daylight during the daytime instead of artificial lighting, which can save energy.


**Limiting Water Heater Usage**

:

When taking showers, use warm water sparingly, as heating water consumes a significant amount of energy.




**Supporting Renewable Energy**:

:

Learn about and support renewable energy sources like solar and wind power. Encourage your family and community to invest in renewable energy solutions.




**Promoting Public Transportation**

:

Encourage the use of public transportation, carpooling, or biking instead of personal cars to reduce the energy and emissions associated with transportation.

**Tree Plantation**

:

Participate in tree plantation drives and promote afforestation, as trees help reduce the energy required for cooling through shade and transpiration.

**Water and Energy Efficiency**

:

Advocate for the installation of energy-efficient appliances and water-saving devices in your home to reduce energy and water consumption.




**Community Initiatives**:

:

Get involved in community projects related to energy conservation and sustainable practices, such as setting up community solar panels or energy-efficient street lighting.
