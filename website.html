EARTH HAVENS

UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and Indian Traditions

Step into a world where the ancient melodies of Indian traditions harmonize with the contemporary beats of global sustainability. Calling all young minds aged 10-15 to join us on an extraordinary expedition that bridges the gap between heritage and modernity, as we unravel the potential for change encapsulated in the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

In the heart of India's cultural tapestry lies a profound wisdom that has withstood the test of time – a wisdom that beckons the youth to channel their energy into positive action. Our journey is an invitation to explore this intersection, where the vibrant threads of tradition weave seamlessly into the fabric of the SDGs. As the custodians of tomorrow, you hold the key to unlocking a future where age-old values propel us toward sustainable solutions.

Let's embark on a voyage where the past guides the present, and where the echo of Indian traditions resonates with the global call for a more sustainable and inclusive world. Embrace the opportunity to make a lasting impact on the canvas of SDGs, drawing inspiration from the cultural mosaic that is uniquely Indian. Welcome to a convergence of heritage and hope – where your actions today sculpt the contours of a brighter, more sustainable future.







Our Mission

"Our mission is to empower students with knowledge and awareness about Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) in the Indian traditions. We aim to provide educational resources and information that not only inform but also inspire students to make a positive impact in their communities, fostering a deeper understanding of the rich cultural heritage and global sustainability efforts. Together let us make our Earth a Haven for us and future generations "
